About

JOCADE is an online mini arcade website that offers users the opportunity to play games and earn points. The site features a wide variety of popular arcade games, allowing players to relive their childhood memories while also earning rewards. With the points they earn, users can purchase items from the JOCADE marketplace, adding an extra level of excitement to the gaming experience. The website was created with the aim of providing the best online arcade experience possible, and it is the passion of its creator for arcade games that drives this mission.
