JOCOINS is an exciting new point system that allows users to earn rewards by playing a variety of mini games. With JOCOINS, users can accumulate points that can be redeemed for cool prizes in the marketplace. The prizes available in the marketplace are displayed on each user's account panel for others to see, making the competition for JOCOINS more intense. The earning of JOCOINS is simple, by participating and performing well in the mini games, users can earn points that can be used to purchase items in the marketplace. The mini games offer a variety of challenges and are designed to be both fun and competitive. The more points users earn, the more they can purchase in the marketplace, giving users the opportunity to showcase their skills and compete against each other for the coolest prizes.
